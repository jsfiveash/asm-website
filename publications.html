ASM Publications

For more than 60 years, the Archeological Society of Maryland, Inc. has aggressively supported a strong publications program.

ASM Ink - The Society Newsletter

ASM Ink, sent electronically each month is an 8 to 12-page newsletter that keeps every member of the Society abreast of the latest archeological news in Maryland...field and lab projects needing volunteers...upcoming meetings, workshops, and talks...Chapter news...legislation and other action affecting archeology...and much, much more. Since March 2002 the newsletter has been available to ASM members on-line. The ASM Board of Directors recently decided to make it available to the general public through this website. Use the selection tool, below to view current and past issues of ASM Ink.

Items for the newsletter should be directed to the Editor, Kim Malinowski.

Maryland Archeology

Maryland Archeology, ASM's flagship journal, is the only publication dedicated to archeology in the state of Maryland. The journal is published biannually and is available to all readers. An excellent 50-year index to the journal has been compiled by Dennis Curry and is available on our Journal webpage.

All correspondence and manuscripts for the journal can be sent to the Editor at Matthew.McKnight@maryland.gov.
