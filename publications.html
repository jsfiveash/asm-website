ASM Publications

For more than 30 years, the Archeological Society of Maryland, Inc. has aggressively supported a strong publications program.

ASM Ink - The Society Newsletter

ASM Ink, sent electronically each month is an 8 to 12-page newsletter that keeps every member of the Society abreast of the latest archeological news in Maryland...field and lab projects needing volunteers...upcoming meetings, workshops, and talks...Chapter news...legislation and other action affecting archeology...and much, much more. Since March 2002 the newsletter has been available to ASM members on-line. The ASM Board of Directors recently decided to make it available to the general public through this website. Use the selection tool, below to view current and past issues of ASM Ink. Items for the newsletter should be directed to the Editor, Myron Beckenstein.

Maryland Archeology

Maryland Archeology, is ASM's journal and is available to ASM members via JOOMAG.COM. The journal is published biannually.

Maryland Archeology Access Instructions

In 2020, the ASM Board voted to transition Maryland Archeology to an entirely digital and online publication platform. This will save the ASM a considerable amount of money in printing and mailing costs. With the help of MHT, the ASM was able to have all of the past print editions (55 years worth) scanned and converted to digital files. This has allowed the organization to upload the full library of past journal issues for access by the membership. The system we are using is called Joomag Newsstand and each active member of the ASM receives free access to the platform as a benefit of membership.

You can sign up for a free Joomag Newsstand account using the email address you have registered with ASM. Please follow the steps below to create a Joomag Newsstand account, activate it, and access Maryland Archeology.

  1. Using your web browser navigate to the JOOMAG Sign-up Page.
  2. Enter your name and the email address you registered with ASM (contact the Membership Secretary if uncertain). Create a strong password, check the CAPTCHA box and click "Sign Up". NOTE: If at any point you are taken to a link to create a "Business Account" or "Education Account", do not create one. The link above should take you directly to the sign-up for a free Joomag Newsstand "Reader Account”.
  3. You should receive an email confirmation for your reader account directly from Joomag. The message will ask you to activate your account. Do so by clicking the "Activate" button in the email. NOTE: Be sure to check your junk mail folder for the Joomag activation letter if it seems delayed.
  4. After activating you should be able to access the full Maryland Archeology library going forward at the link below:
  • https://newsstand.joomag.com/en/my-library
  • Currently, only “born digital” issues of the journal (from the year 2000 on) are individually keyword searchable. We hope to add more functionality in the future.
