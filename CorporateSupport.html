Become a member of ASM today

Membership in the Archeological Society of Maryland, Inc. opens a window to Maryland's past.

Among the immediate benefits of ASM membership, you will:

  • Learn about upcoming events and issues from ASM Ink, a monthly newsletter.
  • Receive the biannual journal Maryland Archeology, detailing exciting discoveries.
  • Attend Society events at reduced admission.
  • Receive a discount on publications, event fees, hand tools, T-shirts, and other items sold by the Society.

You may also participate in numerous activities and events throughout the year, including:

  • The Annual Field Session in Maryland Archeology, a yearly, professionally directed, excavation on a Maryland archeological site.
  • The Annual Fall Meeting and the Annual Spring Symposium.
  • The Annual Workshop in Archeology.
  • Ongoing, professionally directed, volunteer activities including excavations, processing and cataloging artifacts, and other laboratory tasks.
  • Local Chapter meetings and sponsored events.
  • A wide variety of lectures, displays, and hands-on activities during the statewide Maryland Archeology Month celebration.
